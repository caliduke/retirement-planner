2026 Strategy Guide

1. The 12% "Sweet Spot"

For 2026, the 12% Fed bracket for married seniors ends at roughly $100,800 of taxable income. Beyond this, every dollar is taxed at 22%. Your goal is to fill this 12% bucket with 401k/RRSP withdrawals first.

2. Medicare IRMAA Radar

Surcharges hit at $218,000 MAGI. If you cross this by $1, you and your spouse will pay an extra ~$1,950 per year in premiums.

While the IRS taxes 85% of your benefits, California taxes 0%. The app automatically splits this logic to ensure your CA state tax stays accurate.
