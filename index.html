STRATEGIC ACTION PLAN (2026)

1. The Triple Bucket Strategy

To maximize your net income, you must draw from your three "buckets" in a specific order of priority:

  • Bucket 1 (401k / RRSP): Ordinary Income. Use this to fill your 12% Fed bracket. For 2026, stay under $100,800 of taxable income to avoid the 22% jump.
  • Bucket 2 (Brokerage / Index): Capital Gains. These are taxed more lightly. Use this to "bridge the gap" for extra spending without pushing into high tax brackets.
  • Bucket 3 (Roth / HSA): 100% Tax-Free. This is your "Safety Valve." Use it for extra cash when you are already at the IRMAA limit.
2. The 2026 IRMAA Cliffs

Medicare Part B and D premiums are based on your Modified Adjusted Gross Income (MAGI) from two years prior. Crossing a "cliff" by $1 triggers the full surcharge:

  • Safe Zone: Under $218,000 (Joint).
  • Tier 1 Surcharge: $218,000 - $274,000 (Adds ~$2,300/year for a couple).
  • Tier 2 Surcharge: $274,000 - $342,000 (Adds ~$5,770/year for a couple).
3. The California Social Security Shield

While the Federal government taxes up to 85% of your Social Security benefits, California is one of the states that taxes 0%. This app is custom-programmed to shield your SS benefits from CA state tax calculations automatically.

4. How to use the "Bucket Fill"

Watch the bottom-right tracker. If it says "22% BUCKET FILL", you have likely over-drawn from your 401k. Switch some of that withdrawal to the Roth/HSA field until you move back into the 12% zone.
