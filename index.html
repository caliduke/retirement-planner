Master Strategy Guide

1. The Triple Bucket Strategy

Coordinate your withdrawals to maximize net "Efficiency":

  • Ordinary (401k): Fill the 12% Fed bracket (up to $100,800 taxable).
  • Capital Gains (Brokerage): High efficiency for extra spending.
  • Tax-Free (Roth): Shield against IRMAA cliffs.

2. 2026 IRMAA Cliffs

Stay under $218,000 (Joint) to avoid the annual surcharge. Crossing this adds ~$2,300/year to your premiums.

3. California Tax Shield

California taxes 0% of Social Security. This app excludes SS from CA calculations automatically.
